Section 04

PAYMENTS EXPLAINED

Players pay online via Razorpay (UPI, card, net banking). There are two payment types — Full and Advance.

💰
Full Payment
Player pays the entire slot amount online before arriving.
  • ✓ 100% collected online
  • ✓ Nothing to collect at venue
  • ✓ Status shows "Fully Paid"
🔖
Advance Payment
Player pays a small advance online. You collect the rest at the turf.
  • ✓ Advance collected online
  • ✓ You collect remaining at venue
  • ✓ Mark "Collected" in app when done
💡 Example — Advance booking, 2 slots at ₹400/hr
Slot price (2 × ₹400)₹800
Advance paid online by player₹200
You collect at venue₹600
Total you receive₹800 ✓
💡
Marking remaining as collected
In your Revenue screen, advance bookings show a green "Mark Collected" button. Once the player pays the rest at your turf, tap it. The record updates and it disappears from your pending list.

🏠
Home
Today's booking count, this week's revenue, pending remaining payments, upcoming bookings, and a 7-day slot overview with date picker.

Bookings
All your bookings in one place — Today, Upcoming, and Past tabs. See player name, slot time, payment type, and amount for each.

Section 06

BLOCKING SLOTS

Need to close a time slot for maintenance, a private event, or any reason? Block it manually — blocked slots show as "Locked" to players and can't be booked.

1
Go to Home → Block Slots
Find the Block Slots option from your Home or Bookings screen quick actions.
2
Select the date
Pick which date you want to block slots on.
3
Pick the time slot(s)
Choose which 30-minute slots to lock. Already-booked slots cannot be blocked.
4
Confirm — done instantly
The slot is immediately marked yellow "Locked" for all players on the Explore screen.
✅
Your operator can block slots too
If you have an on-ground operator, they can also block slots from their phone — useful when they spot a maintenance issue at the venue.
Section 07

TURF OPERATORS

An operator is your on-ground staff member — the person physically present at the turf. They get their own login with limited, job-focused access.

✅
Operators CAN
  • ✓ See today's bookings
  • ✓ See player name and payment status
  • ✓ Block time slots
  • ✓ View live slot availability
🚫
Operators CANNOT
  • ✕ See your revenue or earnings
  • ✕ Edit turf details or pricing
  • ✕ See past or future bookings
  • ✕ Cancel or modify bookings
👤
How to set up an operator
Send us your operator's name, email, and phone number. We create their account and link it to your turf. They download the same Playon app, log in, and automatically see the Operator screen — no role selection needed.
Section 08

CANCELLATIONS

Players can cancel their own bookings through the app. Refunds are processed automatically — you don't need to do anything.

ScenarioPlayer refundYou receive
Cancelled 2+ hours before slotFull refund (100%)₹0
Cancelled within 2 hours of slotNo refundAdvance amount kept
Past bookings (date passed)Cannot be cancelled—
♻️
Cancelled slots re-open automatically
When a player cancels, their slot is freed up within seconds — other players can book it immediately. Nothing for you to do.
⏱️
Refunds take 5–7 business days
Razorpay processes refunds to the player's original payment method. If a player asks about their refund status, this is normal.
