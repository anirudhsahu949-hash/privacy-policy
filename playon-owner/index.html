Section 04

PAYMENTS

Players pay via Razorpay (UPI, card, net banking). Two types — Full and Advance.

💰
Full Payment
  • ✓ Player pays 100% online
  • ✓ Nothing to collect at venue
  • ✓ Status shows "Fully Paid"
🔖
Advance Payment
  • ✓ Player pays advance online
  • ✓ You collect remaining at venue
  • ✓ Tap "Mark Collected" in app when done
💡 Example — Advance booking, 2 slots at ₹400/hr
Slot price (2 × ₹400)₹800
Advance paid online₹200
You collect at venue₹600
Total you receive₹800 ✓
💡
How to mark remaining as collected
In your Revenue screen, advance bookings show a green "Mark Collected" button. Tap it once the player pays you at the turf — the record updates and clears from your pending list.

Section 06

BLOCKING SLOTS

Need to close a time slot for maintenance or a private event? Block it — blocked slots show as "Locked" to players and can't be booked.

1
Go to Home → Block Slots
Find Block Slots from your Home or Bookings screen quick actions.
2
Select the date
Pick which date you want to block slots on.
3
Pick the time slot(s)
Choose the 30-minute slots to lock. Already-booked slots cannot be blocked.
4
Confirm — done instantly
Slot immediately shows as yellow "Locked" for all players on the Explore screen.
✅
Your operator can block slots too
Useful when they spot a maintenance issue at the venue and need to close a slot on the spot.
Section 07

OPERATORS

An operator is your on-ground staff — the person physically at the turf. They get their own limited login.

✅
Operators CAN
  • ✓ See today's bookings
  • ✓ See player name and payment status
  • ✓ Block time slots
  • ✓ View live slot availability
🚫
Operators CANNOT
  • ✕ See your revenue or earnings
  • ✕ Edit turf details or pricing
  • ✕ See past or future bookings
  • ✕ Cancel or modify bookings
👤
How to set up an operator
Send us their name, email, and phone. We create their account and link it to your turf. They download Playon, log in, and automatically see the Operator screen.
Section 08

CANCELLATIONS

Players can cancel themselves through the app. Refunds are automatic — you don't need to do anything.

✅ Cancelled 2+ hours before slot
Player gets full refund. You receive ₹0.
⏰ Cancelled within 2 hours of slot
No refund to player. You keep the advance amount.
📅 Past bookings (date already passed)
Cannot be cancelled.
ScenarioPlayer refundYou receive
Cancelled 2+ hours before slotFull refund (100%)₹0
Cancelled within 2 hoursNo refundAdvance amount kept
Past bookingsCannot be cancelled—
♻️
Cancelled slots re-open automatically
Freed up within seconds — other players can book immediately. Nothing for you to do.
⏱️
Refunds take 5–7 business days
Razorpay processes refunds to the player's original payment method. This is normal — if a player asks, reassure them.
