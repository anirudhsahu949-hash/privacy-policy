Section 01
What is Playon?
Playon is a sports turf booking platform that connects players with turf venues. Players discover, book, and pay for your turf — all through the app. You receive the booking, manage your schedule, and collect payment.
📱
Mobile-First App
Available on Android. Players book directly from their phone 24/7, even when you're asleep.
💳
Online Payments
Secure payments via Razorpay — UPI, cards, net banking all supported. Money is tracked automatically.
📅
Live Slot Calendar
Real-time slot availability — when a player books a slot, it's instantly marked as taken for everyone.
🔔
Instant Notifications
You get a push notification the moment a player books your turf — name, date, slot, and amount.
📊
Revenue Dashboard
See your earnings for the last 7, 15, or 30 days with a breakdown by turf and booking type.
🏟️
Multi-Turf Support
Own multiple turfs? All of them appear under one owner account with separate tracking.
Section 02
The Four Roles
Playon has four types of accounts. Each sees a completely different screen when they log in.
🏃
Player (User)
Anyone who wants to book a turf. They browse venues, pick time slots, and pay online.
  • Browse all turfs on the Explore screen
  • See real-time slot availability
  • Book and pay via Razorpay
  • View and cancel their bookings
  • Contact support if needed
🏢
Turf Owner (You)
The owner of the turf venue. You manage turf info, monitor bookings, and track revenue.
  • See today's and upcoming bookings
  • Track revenue with date filters
  • Edit turf details and pricing
  • Block specific slots
  • Assign an on-ground operator
  • Mark remaining payments as collected
🎽
Turf Operator
Your on-ground staff present at the turf. They see who's coming today and can block slots.
  • See today's bookings only
  • View player names and payment status
  • Block slots (maintenance, etc.)
  • Cannot see revenue or edit turf
⚙️
Platform Admin
The Playon team. They add new turfs, manage owner accounts, and run the platform.
  • Add / remove / edit any turf
  • Manage all owner accounts
  • View all bookings across all turfs
  • Send platform-wide notifications
  • Manage promotional banners
ℹ️
Your login
The Playon admin creates your owner account and sends you your login email and password. Once you log in, the app automatically shows you the Owner screen — you don't need to select any role yourself.

Two Payment Types

💰
Full Payment
Player pays the complete slot price online before arriving.
  • 100% collected online via Razorpay
  • No remaining amount at venue
  • Status shows "Fully Paid"
  • Refundable if cancelled before 2 hours
🔖
Advance Payment
Player pays a partial advance online and pays the rest at the turf.
  • Advance amount set by admin (e.g. ₹200)
  • Remaining collected at venue by you
  • You mark "Remaining Collected" in app
  • Only advance is refunded if cancelled
💡 Example: Advance booking for 2 slots at ₹400/hr
Slot price (2 × ₹400)₹800
Advance paid online₹200
Remaining (collect at venue)₹600
Total you receive₹800
✅
Marking remaining as collected
In your Revenue screen, each advance booking shows a "Mark Collected" button. Once the player pays the remaining amount at the venue, tap this button to update the record. This keeps your revenue tracking accurate.

Section 06
Revenue & Earnings
The Revenue tab gives you a complete financial picture — how much has been collected, how much is pending at the venue, and which bookings are outstanding.
1
Choose your date range
Tap the date filter at the top — Last 7 Days, Last 15 Days, or Last 30 Days. The numbers update instantly.
2
Read the summary cards
Total Revenue = all confirmed bookings. Online Collected = paid via Razorpay. Remaining = advance bookings where you still need to collect at venue. Cancelled = refunded bookings.
3
Scroll the booking list
Each booking card shows player name, date, slot, payment type, and amounts. Tap any card for full details.
4
Mark remaining as collected
For advance bookings, you'll see a green "Mark Collected" button. Once the player pays you at the turf, tap this. The booking updates to "fully collected" and won't appear in your pending list.

Section 07
Managing Your Turf
Your turf listing is what players see in the Explore screen. Keep it updated for maximum bookings.

What Players See on Your Turf Card

🖼️
Turf Photos
Multiple images shown in a gallery. First image is the cover photo. Use real, well-lit photos — they increase bookings significantly.
📍
Name & Location
Your turf name and area are shown on every card. Players use this to find turfs near them.
⚽
Sports
The sports your turf supports (Football, Cricket, etc.). Players filter by sport — make sure yours are listed correctly.
💰
Pricing
Day price (6 AM–6 PM) and Night price (6 PM–12 AM) shown on the card. Advance booking price is how much players pay online.
🕐
Opening Hours
The hours your turf is available for booking. Slots are only shown within this window.
🟢
Live Slot Status
Available (green), Booked (red), Blocked (yellow) slots shown in real-time so players know exactly what's free.
✏️
To edit your turf details
Contact the Playon admin to update your turf name, pricing, opening hours, photos, or sports. These changes take effect immediately across the app. In a future update, you'll be able to edit some details yourself from the Profile tab.

Section 08
Slot Blocking
Need to close specific time slots for maintenance, a private event, or any reason? You can block slots manually — blocked slots appear as yellow "Locked" chips to players and cannot be booked.
1
Go to Bookings tab → Block Slots
This is accessible from the Bookings screen in your owner dashboard.
2
Select the date
Pick the date you want to block slots on using the date picker.
3
Choose the time slot
Select the specific 30-minute slot(s) you want to lock. Already-booked slots cannot be blocked.
4
Confirm the block
The slot is immediately marked as "Locked" (yellow) for all players on the Explore screen.
✅
Your operator can also block slots
Your assigned turf operator has access to the same slot-blocking feature. This is useful when your operator is at the venue and needs to close a slot on the spot (e.g. maintenance issue, field damage).

Section 10
Turf Operators
A turf operator is your on-ground staff member who physically manages the turf. They get a separate login with limited access — only what they need to do their job.
✅
What Operators CAN do
  • See today's bookings for this turf
  • See player name and contact
  • See payment status (paid / remaining)
  • Block specific time slots
  • View slot availability in real-time
🚫
What Operators CANNOT do
  • See revenue or earnings
  • Edit turf details or pricing
  • See past or future bookings
  • Access your profile or settings
  • Cancel or modify bookings
👤
How to assign an operator
Contact the Playon admin with your operator's name, email, and phone number. The admin will create their account and link them to your turf. Your operator then logs into the same Playon app with their own credentials and automatically sees the Operator screen.
Section 11
Cancellations & Refunds
Players can cancel their bookings themselves through the app. Refunds are handled automatically by the system — you don't need to do anything.

Cancellation Rules

ScenarioRefund to PlayerYou Receive
Cancelled more than 2 hours before slotFull refund (100%)₹0 for that booking
Advance booking cancelled earlyAdvance amount refunded₹0 (no money collected yet)
Cancelled within 2 hours of slotNo refund (non-refundable)Advance amount is kept
Past bookings (date already passed)Cannot be cancelled—
♻️
Cancelled slots re-open automatically
When a player cancels, their slot is immediately freed up and becomes available for other players to book. You don't need to do anything — the system handles this automatically within seconds.
⚠️
Refunds take 5–7 business days
Razorpay processes refunds back to the player's original payment method. This typically takes 5–7 business days depending on their bank. If a player asks about their refund, this is normal and expected.
